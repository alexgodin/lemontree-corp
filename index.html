We believe everyone deserves an amazing dinner.

In a previous life we sold family dinner (more in the Wall Street Journal).

As the COVID-19 pandemic struck New York, our nimble startup team pivoted to help save NYC's struggling emergency food system. Our first project, In It Together, connects thousands of volunteers with more than 70 high-need organizations. Now, we're expanding and continuing our work.

As the next months and years unfold, we're commited to making dinner time more dignified, more delicious and more fun for low-income families. As we do this, we're big believers in the power of techonolgy and systems that scale. And we know that the best solutions come from listening to people who aren't like us.

Our team is supported by an incredible family of donors and we'd be absolutly honored if you would join them by making a donation.
